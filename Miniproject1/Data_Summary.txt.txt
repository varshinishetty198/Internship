Dataset Name: Customer Analytics Dataset

Number of Rows: 255
Number of Columns: 14

Data Types
Numerical Columns:CustomerID, Age, AnnualIncome, SpendingScore, YearsEmployed, PurchaseFrequency, OnlineVisitsPerMonth, ReturnedItems, LastPurchaseAmount
Categorical Columns:Gender, City, Education, MaritalStatus, PreferredDevice

Summary
Each row in the dataset represents a single customer.
The dataset contains both demographic details (such as age, gender, education, and marital status) and behavioral information (such as spending score, purchase frequency, and last purchase amount).
